Your Artwork

Three layers, one clean print.

If you only remember one rule: keep critical text inside the safe zone, push backgrounds out to the bleed.

  • Bleed Line — +3mm outside trim Extend background colors and images this far past the cut. Prevents white edges if the cutter shifts.
  • Trim Line — final cut edge Where the blade hits. Your finished piece ends here.
  • Safe Zone — 3mm inside trim All critical text, logos and important detail must live here. Anything outside risks getting cut.
