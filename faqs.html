Frequently Asked Questions

The questions we hear most often — and the straight answers.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQs vary by product and process:

Offset printing & foil stamping: typically 500 pcs.
Diecut packaging: from 300 pcs.
UV print on merchandise (tumblers, USB): from 50 pcs.
Screen printing (totebags, ecobags): from 100 pcs.
Layout & design only: no minimum.

If your run is below MOQ, message us anyway — we may be able to combine your job with another scheduled run.

How long does production take?

Standard turnaround depends on the product and finishing:

Simple print (flyers, cards): 5–7 business days.
Offset boxes & paperbags: 7–10 business days.
Diecut + foil + emboss: 10–14 business days.
Custom packaging development: 14–21 business days (includes dieline & sample).

Rush production may be available with a surcharge — ask when you request a quote.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes — every quote is free. Send us your specs (product, size, quantity, finish, deadline) via the contact form, email or WhatsApp. We typically respond within one business day.

What file formats should I send for printing?

We accept the following print-ready formats:

Preferred: PDF (with bleed and crop marks), Adobe Illustrator (.AI), Photoshop (.PSD).
Acceptable: high-resolution JPG / PNG (300 DPI minimum at print size).

If you only have a rough mockup or sketch, our in-house design team can prepare print-ready artwork for you (free with most print orders).

What is bleed and why does it matter?

Bleed is the area of artwork that extends beyond the final cut edge (typically 3mm on each side). It ensures no white edges appear if the cutter shifts slightly. Always include bleed on artwork that has color or images running to the edge.

If you're not sure how to set this up, send us your file as-is — we'll fix it during pre-press.

Can you design my packaging or layout for me?

Yes. We have an in-house layout & design team. Send us your concept, brand assets (logo, colors), and a brief — we'll create the artwork, send a digital proof for your approval, and revise until you're happy.

Layout work is typically free or discounted when bundled with a print order.

Do you provide a sample or proof before printing?

Yes. For every job we send a digital proof (PDF) showing the final layout for your approval before going to press. For larger runs or custom packaging, we can also provide a physical mockup or pre-production sample at additional cost.

We never start production without your written sign-off.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

GCash, Maya, BPI, BDO, Metrobank bank transfers, and cash for pickups.

Standard terms: 50% downpayment to start production, 50% balance before delivery / pickup. Repeat clients may qualify for net-15 or net-30 terms.

Do you deliver outside Metro Manila?

Yes — we ship nationwide. Within Metro Manila we can arrange same-day or next-day courier delivery (Lalamove, Grab Express). For provincial orders we use LBC, J&T or your preferred courier. Shipping cost is billed at actual or prepaid by the client.

Pickup at our Quezon City office is also free.

Can you match a specific Pantone or brand color?

Yes. We work with Pantone (PMS) color references and CMYK process colors. For brand-critical jobs, share your Pantone code or a printed sample for color matching. Slight variation between digital and printed output is normal — we'll let you know if a color is outside our reproduction range.

Do you offer eco-friendly or sustainable options?

Yes — recyclable kraft paper, recycled chipboard for boxes, and water-based inks where applicable. Tell us your sustainability goals when requesting a quote and we'll recommend material options that fit.

Can I reorder previous designs?

Absolutely. We keep all approved artwork on file. To reorder, just message us with the project name or previous order reference and the new quantity — no need to resend artwork.

Do you handle bulk corporate orders or recurring contracts?

Yes. We're set up for recurring monthly or quarterly print runs (calendars, packaging, marketing collateral). Volume discounts apply for committed orders. Contact us to discuss a supply agreement.
