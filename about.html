About LLS Printing

A locally-rooted print company built on craftsmanship, color accuracy and quiet reliability.

Our Story

From small workshop to trusted partner.

LLS Printing Services Inc. began in April 2019 as a sole proprietorship (formerly Liya Averie) and has since grown into an incorporated company serving brands locally and nationwide. The foundation behind LLS — almost 25 years of hands-on experience in offset printing and packaging — is what continues to drive every job that leaves our press.

We specialize in custom-volume packaging, commercial print runs and finishing work that other shops outsource. From food boxes to hardbound books, we treat every order as a brand touchpoint, not a commodity.
